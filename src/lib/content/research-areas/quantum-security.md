With the future advent of quantum computing, traditional asymmetric cryptography (such as RSA and ECC) will be broken. Our research focuses on implementing quantum cryptographic systems to secure vehicular and medical IoT networks. We specialize in Continuous-Variable Quantum Key Distribution (CV-QKD) architectures.

### Active Investigations
- **High-Mobility Key Agreement**: Resolving keys under rapid channel fluctuations, such as dynamic vehicle-to-everything (V2X) nodes.
- **Quantum-Secured Hashing**: Constructing tamper-proof network signatures using quantum key generation rate hashes.
- **Edge Gateway Decryption**: Implementing symmetric AES pipelines wrapped in CV-QKD key agreements on low-power IoT gateway architectures.
