Large Language Models (LLMs) are transforming how humans interact with structured databases and business telemetry. Our research focuses on structuring reliable, secure LLM agents for operational tools. We investigate Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and translation pipelines converting natural language questions into executable database queries (NL-to-SQL).

### Active Investigations
- **Secure Text-to-SQL Sandbox**: Designing strict prompt guidelines and read-only database configurations to prevent SQL injection through LLM interfaces.
- **Voice Agent Integration**: Pair voice transcription networks (such as Whisper) with LLM agent systems (like LangGraph) for low-latency voice assistance.
- **Acronym and Schema Mapping**: Training vector store indexes to map domain-specific abbreviations and metadata dynamically to database columns.
