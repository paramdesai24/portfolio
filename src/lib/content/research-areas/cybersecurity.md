Modern infrastructures depend on interconnected IoT gateways and autonomous communication networks. These nodes are vulnerable to distributed attacks. Our cybersecurity research builds intelligent, lightweight intrusion detection systems (IDS) to analyze anomalies in telemetry feeds and classify threats in real-time.

### Active Investigations
- **False Data Injection (FDI) Defense**: Building classifiers to isolate spoofed coordinates or falsified sensor telemetry in autonomous vehicle networks.
- **Botnet Attack Classification**: Training low-power classifiers to run on local gateway routers, identifying and block botnet traffic patterns.
- **Secure Aggregation**: Investigating aggregation protocols for federated networks that remain robust against Byzantine node poisoning.
